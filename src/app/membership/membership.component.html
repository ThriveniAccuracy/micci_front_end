Membership Notes

There are three types of membership, Premier Membership, Ordinary Membership and Associate Membership

ORDINARY MEMBERS

Any company registered and authorized to carry out bussiness in Malaysia. A company's Membership Catagory is based on its Issued Capital or NO.of Employees. Should a company quality for more than one membership category,the membership category with a higher annual subscription will apply.

ASSOCIATE MEMBERS

Any association,non-profit organization, public university, or company registered outside of Malaysia. An Associate Member shall have the like rights and duties of an Ordinary Member save that it shall not be entitled to vote at General Meeting of the Chamber nor be appointed or elected as a member of the General Committe.

SUBSCRIPTION PAYMENTS

The annual subscription fees shall be due and payable in advanced on the first day of January each year. A member newly elected during the year shall pay subscription fees for the unexpired portion of such year, calculated from the first day of the month following the month in which the Member is elected. The MICCI Membership follows the calendar year commencing 1 Jan and expirind 31 Dec.
